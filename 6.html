BIOGRAPHY

  • Ethnicity: British Australian
    Languages: English
    Accents: Australian, USA
    Skills: Bodybuilding, Boxing, Nurse, Photography
  • Kevin Priestley was born & raised on the Island of Tasmania, Australia. He lives & works in Melbourne as an Actor & Nurse. Brought up in a small forest in the north east of the Island, his father hosted many gatherings of artists & creative people, during summer he was often visited & influenced by the Rainbow Family. As a child, he would regularly attend gatherings & festivals, where he would assist in the distribution & cooking of the Chapati Indian dish. He farmed sheep with his grandfather & learned basic land- management skills, including bush craft. At an early age he became interested in Photo/Videography & attended homeschool, followed by College & University as an adult.
